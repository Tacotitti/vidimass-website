🎵 Why Video Creations Are Everything

Video Creations (User-Generated Content with your song) are the most important ranking factor for musical success on TikTok. Here's the technical breakdown of how TikTok's algorithm works and why mass posting changes the game.

⚙️ TikTok's Algorithm: 3 Phases

Phase 1: Initial Test Pool

  • ▸ New creation video is shown to 300-500 random users
  • ▸ If engagement rate > 8% → advances to Phase 2
  • ▸ If < 8% → video dies

Phase 2: Interest Graph Expansion

  • ▸ Video is shown to users who like similar sounds/hashtags
  • ▸ With each positive signal (Like, Share, Creation) → broader distribution
  • ▸ Songs with many creations have higher starting chance in Phase 2

Phase 3: Viral Explosion

  • ▸ When critical thresholds are exceeded (e.g., 10.000 creations in 24h)
  • ▸ TikTok activates "Viral Push" mechanism
  • ▸ Song is actively pushed in "Trending Sounds" section
